arroz con leche

this is a traditional hispanic dessert, that i especially enjoy.

it is perfect for a sunny day, or a cozy night in. i honest to god dont really know what else to say about it.

ingredients

  • milk
  • water
  • rice
  • sugar
  • sweetened condensed milk
  • cinnamon sticks

optional add-ins

  • vanilla extract
  • evaporated milk
  • salt

steps

  1. rinse your rice
  2. cook the rice with water and cinnamon approx. 20 mins
  3. add milk and sweetener of choice
  4. simmer and thicken
  5. rest for 15-30 mins and serve chilled
