sushi recipe

tracing back to Southeast Asia, this delicious fish and rice based dish is a staple in many Asiac households.

for simplicity reasons, i will be disclosing the recipe for the Philadelphia sushi roll specifically.

ingredients

  • sushi rice
  • smoked salmon
  • cream cheese
  • avocado

steps

  1. rinse sushi rice and cook accordingly
  2. slice salmon
  3. spread layer of rice
  4. add filings
  5. roll and shape
  6. slice and serve
